# Vesra Partner Knowledge Base

Use this as the source of truth for outreach copy, campaign generation, and response drafts.

## Campaign Focus

Active partner campaigns are controlled by `lead-gen/config/icp_profiles.json`.

Current active partner ICPs:

- HR consultancies.
- Franchise/franchisor partners.

Do not include accountancies, employment law firms, recruitment agencies,
general business consultants, franchise directories, franchise consultants, or
organisations with their own competing HR software platform unless a separate
campaign is created for them.

## Mission

Help HR consultancies thrive in the AI era.

## One-Line Description

Vesra helps HR consultancies offer a branded HR platform to their clients without having to build or maintain software themselves.

For franchise networks, Vesra helps head office gain visibility and consistency
across every location without building or maintaining software in-house.

## Core Value Proposition

Turn HR expertise into recurring software revenue.

## Partner Proposition

Best-fit partners are UK HR consultancies with retained SME client relationships.

Vesra gives those consultancies a branded software layer they can offer alongside advice, policies, documentation, and ongoing HR support.

The partner benefit is a stronger client proposition, better client retention, and an additional recurring revenue stream without increasing consulting hours.

For franchise networks, Vesra supports more consistent holiday management,
sickness tracking, employee records, policy acknowledgements, onboarding,
compliance, reporting, and oversight across locations.

The franchise-network benefit is better operational control, greater visibility,
lower administrative burden, reduced compliance risk, faster onboarding, and a
more consistent employee experience as the network grows.

## Ideal Partner Criteria

- UK based.
- 2-50 employees.
- 20+ active clients.
- Ongoing retained client relationships.
- Provides outsourced HR support.
- Advises SMEs on people, compliance, and employment matters.
- Looking to grow recurring revenue.
- Does not have its own software platform.

## Partner Services

Best-fit HR consultancy partners usually provide:

- HR advice.
- Employee relations.
- Policies and handbooks.
- Performance management.
- Absence management.
- Compliance support.
- Employment documentation.
- HR consultancy and retained services.

## Partner Challenges

- Revenue tied to consulting hours.
- Clients increasingly expect software alongside advice.
- Difficulty differentiating from other consultancies.
- Limited recurring revenue streams.
- Risk of clients moving to larger providers with technology offerings.
- Growing pressure to embrace AI and automation.

## Why Vesra Fits

- HR consultancies already have trusted client relationships.
- They regularly advise clients on HR processes and systems.
- Their clients often ask for software recommendations.
- They can introduce Vesra as part of an existing service offering.
- Vesra can support recurring revenue and client retention.

## Outcomes To Emphasize

- More revenue.
- Better client retention.
- More value for clients.
- Stronger differentiation.
- Reduced reliance on billable hours.
- A more modern service offering.

## Core Belief

Clients increasingly expect software, self-service, and AI-powered experiences alongside professional advice.

## Suggested Partner Call Agenda

1. Understand the consultancy's client base and retained model.
2. Identify where clients currently ask for HR systems or software.
3. Explain how a branded Vesra partner model could work.
4. Discuss commercial structure and implementation.
5. Agree whether to trial with one or two clients.
